<p><ul><li>Non-Destructive Testing (NDT)</li><li>Universal Testing Machine (UTM)</li><li>Servo Hydraulic Testing Machine (SHTM)</li><li>Hardness Testing Machine (HTM)</li><li>Impact Testing Machine (ITM)</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Construction Material Testing Equipment market comprises several key types. Non-Destructive Testing (NDT) assesses materials without causing damage, ensuring structural integrity. Universal Testing Machines (UTM) evaluate tensile and compressive strength. Servo Hydraulic Testing Machines (SHTM) are used for precise control in testing materials under various conditions. Hardness Testing Machines (HTM) determine material hardness, while Impact Testing Machines (ITM) measure energy absorption during fractures. Other types include advanced equipment for specific reactions, enhancing material assessment and quality control in construction.</p></p>
