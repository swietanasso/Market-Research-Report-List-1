<p><p>The digital coaxial cable market is primarily categorized into two interface types: RCA and BNC. The RCA interface is widely used in consumer electronics for audio and video signals, offering a simple connection method. In contrast, the BNC interface is common in professional settings, such as broadcasting and security systems, due to its superior durability and locking mechanism. Both types serve specific applications, with RCA favored for home use and BNC preferred in professional and industrial environments.</p></p>
