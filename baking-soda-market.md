<p><p>The baking soda market comprises three main types: soda ash, double decomposition, and natural soda. Soda ash is primarily produced through the Solvay process, widely used in various industries, including glass manufacturing. Double decomposition involves a chemical reaction where sodium carbonate is created from sodium bicarbonate. Natural soda, derived from mineral deposits, is obtained through evaporation of brine or mining of trona ore. Each type serves unique applications, contributing to diverse market demands in industrial and consumer sectors.</p></p>
