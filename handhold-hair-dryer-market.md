<p><p>The handheld hair dryer market primarily features two types: centrifugal and axial hair dryers. Centrifugal hair dryers utilize a fan to draw air in and push it out, creating a strong airflow for rapid drying. They are known for their efficiency and powerful performance. In contrast, axial hair dryers employ a straight-line airflow design, producing a gentler drying experience. This makes them suitable for more delicate hair types. Both types cater to diverse consumer preferences in hair styling and drying needs.</p></p>
