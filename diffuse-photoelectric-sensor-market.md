<p><p>The diffuse photoelectric sensor market includes two primary types: red light sensors and laser sensors. Red light sensors utilize visible red LED light to detect objects based on reflection, ideal for non-contact applications in standard environments. In contrast, laser sensors emit focused beams of laser light, enabling precise detection over longer distances and in challenging conditions. Both types are essential in automation, material handling, and safety applications, catering to diverse industrial needs with varying levels of accuracy and distance capabilities.</p></p>
