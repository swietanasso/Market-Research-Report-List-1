<p><p>The amber glass vials market is primarily categorized into two types: tube-made bottles and molded bottles. Tube-made bottles are produced by heating and blowing molten glass into a tube shape, offering uniformity and clarity, making them ideal for pharmaceuticals and laboratory use. Molded bottles, on the other hand, are created by pouring molten glass into pre-formed molds, allowing for more complex designs and larger sizes. Both types provide protection from light, making them suitable for sensitive substances.</p></p>
