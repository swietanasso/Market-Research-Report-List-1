<p><p>The overhead conveyors market comprises various types, including Suspension Chain Conveyors, Push Type Suspension Chain Conveyors, and Drag Type Suspension Chain Conveyors. Suspension Chain Conveyors utilize chains suspended from a track to transport items overhead, optimizing floor space. Push Type systems operate by pushing loads along the chain, suitable for lighter objects. In contrast, Drag Type Conveyors are designed for heavier loads, utilizing a chain to pull materials along, making them ideal for industrial applications requiring robust handling solutions.</p></p>
