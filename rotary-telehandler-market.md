<p><p>The rotary telehandler market features three main steering types: Two-Wheel Steering, Four-Wheel Steering, and Crab Steering. Two-Wheel Steering offers improved maneuverability in tight spaces, making it suitable for smaller sites. Four-Wheel Steering enhances stability and agility, allowing for better handling on uneven terrain and larger areas. Crab Steering provides lateral movement, enabling the telehandler to move diagonally, which is beneficial for navigating crowded job sites. Each type caters to specific operational needs and environments, enhancing overall efficiency.</p></p>
