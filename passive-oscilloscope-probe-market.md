<p><p>The passive oscilloscope probe market comprises various attenuation factors, notably 1×, 10×, and 100× probes. 1× probes provide direct signal measurement with no attenuation, ideal for accurate low-frequency signals. 10× probes attenuate the signal by a factor of 10, offering improved bandwidth and reduced loading effects, suitable for general-purpose use. 100× probes further minimize loading, designed for high-frequency applications. Additionally, the "Others" category includes specialized probes for niche applications, enhancing measurement flexibility across diverse electronic testing scenarios.</p></p>
