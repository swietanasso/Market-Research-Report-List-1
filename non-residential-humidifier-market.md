<p><strong>The Non-residential Humidifier Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Adiabatic</li><li>Isothermal</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The non-residential humidifier market primarily consists of two types: adiabatic and isothermal humidifiers. Adiabatic humidifiers work on the principle of evaporative cooling, where water is vaporized into the air without heating it, making them energy-efficient and environmentally friendly. In contrast, isothermal humidifiers maintain air temperature as moisture is added, often using steam generation or hot water. Both types serve different commercial applications, addressing specific humidity needs in various environments like offices, warehouses, and manufacturing facilities.</p></p>
