<p><p>The home solar power system market is categorized into three main types: pitched roof systems, flat roof systems, and ground-mounted systems. Pitched roof systems maximize sunlight exposure with angled installations on sloped roofs, making them ideal for residential spaces. Flat roof systems utilize horizontal surfaces, allowing for easy customization and optimal panel placement. Ground-mounted systems are installed on the ground, suitable for properties with ample land, providing maximum energy output without roof limitations. Each system offers distinct advantages based on property characteristics.</p></p>
