<p><p>The electric torque wrench market is primarily divided into two types: straight type and angled type electric torque wrenches. Straight type electric torque wrenches are designed for applications that require direct access to fasteners, providing a straightforward approach for tightening and loosening tasks. In contrast, angled type electric torque wrenches feature a bend in the shaft, allowing for better maneuverability in tight spaces and hard-to-reach areas, making them ideal for complex assembly tasks in automotive and industrial applications.</p></p>
